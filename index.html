ASTRA

From detection to precision — smarter crop care with minimal chemicals.
Intelligent Pesticide Sprinkling System powered by AI, Sensors & Automation.

Project Abstract

ASTRA (Agri Smart Tech Robotic Automation) is an innovative agri-tech solution designed to revolutionize modern farming through precision and automation. The system integrates AI-based image processing, IoT-enabled sensors, and robotic control mechanisms to detect plant infections and manage pesticide application intelligently. Using real-time data from cameras and NPK sensors, ASTRA identifies affected crop regions and autonomously sprays pesticides only on those specific areas, thereby minimizing chemical wastage and reducing environmental impact. Powered by a Raspberry Pi microcontroller, the system coordinates various modules including a mobile robotic platform, automated sprinkler unit, and cloud-connected monitoring dashboard. The accompanying ASTRA Mobile App provides farmers with live insights such as infection level, soil health, and pesticide usage, enabling data-driven decisions. By promoting eco-friendly, cost-efficient, and precision-driven agriculture, ASTRA aims to enhance crop yield, maintain soil fertility, and reduce human labor—paving the way for a smarter and more sustainable farming future.

Solution / Working Principle

ASTRA functions in three intelligent layers:

  • Sensing: Multispectral & environmental sensors collect crop health data.
  • Decision: On-board edge ML model detects affected areas.
  • Actuation: Targeted sprayers apply precise pesticide dosage only where needed.
